## The offer in one line
Sit with a small-business owner for ~45 min, find their time-drains, then **prescribe 3–7 off-the-shelf
AI/SaaS tools** that reclaim ~5–10 hrs/week. Charge **$999, money-back if you can't find ≥5 hrs.** It's a
**foot-in-the-door**, not the business — lifetime value climbs via the upsell ladder.

- **Who:** owners of ~2–20 staff, ~$500k–5M revenue. ~95% use no AI beyond ChatGPT prompting → blue ocean.
- **Positioning:** "we prescribe, we don't build" — accessible to non-technical operators.

## The 4 phases
1. **Discovery call** (Zoom/Meet + an AI note-taker — Fathom / Otter / Fireflies). *Probing only, no pitching.*
   Question bank:
   - "Walk me through yesterday."
   - "What tasks do you dread?"
   - "Where does work pile up / what's the bottleneck?"
   - "What have you tried to automate and failed?"
   - **The killer:** *"If you could wave a magic wand and delete one process, what would it be?"* (usual answer: email).
   - **Discipline:** bite your tongue — do **not** prescribe on the call.
2. **AI analysis of the transcript** — feed it to Claude (build a **Claude Skill**) to surface pain points +
   research off-the-shelf fixes. **QA step:** don't take the output at face value — substitute inappropriate
   picks (no Salesforce for a 4-person landscaper). **Evals:** feed past transcripts + finished reports back
   into the skill so it "knows what good looks like" (first 2–3 are ~60–70% right; by the 4th–6th, often copy-paste).
   Directories: `futurepedia.io`, `theresanaiforthat.com` (filter by industry).
3. **The report** (the client-facing deliverable; free template at `audittemplate.ai`). Sections:
   - **Executive summary** — 1–2 pain points, outcome, hours reclaimed, the "primary focus."
   - **Effort-vs-impact matrix** — top-left = *quick wins* (high-impact / low-effort = the off-the-shelf tools);
     top-right = *major projects* (= the upsell hooks).
   - **Recommended solutions** — tool · pain point · cost · setup time · time saved.
   - **4-day quick-start plan** — ≤10 min/day (so "a confused mind" still acts).
   - **Financial impact** — net monthly ROI = (weekly hrs reclaimed × hourly rate) − tool cost. Avg tools ~$60/mo → almost always 4-figure ROI.
   - **Mantra:** *"a confused mind doesn't buy, implement, or upsell"* → make it stupid-simple.
   - **"Primary focus"** = one of three ROI levers: **effectiveness** (more money) / **efficiency** (save time) / **quality**.
4. **Review call** (~30 min, screen-share the report). Three closing questions: "Which is most urgent?" ·
   "Do it yourself or want my help?" · "What's your timeline?" → ~50–60% ask for implementation.

## The upsell ladder (the $999 is just the door)
1. **Process redesign** — fix a broken process before automating it (map current → future state; ~$3–3.5k).
2. **Automation build** — Zapier / make / n8n for cut-and-dry 1–3 step flows (~$1.5k).
3. **Knowledge system** — e.g. a custom GPT/agent trained on the business's own material (pulls efficiency + quality levers).
4. **Custom workflows** — Claude Skills + reference files for proprietary processes, with a **recurring maintenance retainer**.

## The funnel maths
**Self-liquidating:** put ~$200 of each $999 into Meta ads → you get *paid* to acquire customers.
